<p>With more than 10,000 acres at our disposal, the Louee Enduro &amp; Motocross Complex has tracks and courses to suit every rider, no matter their age or ability.</p>
<p>Please read the <a href="https://www.louee.net.au/safety-legal" title="We take safety very seriously at Louee.">safety and legal</a> section of this website.</p>
<p>Upon arrival at Louee all riders are asked to sign a waiver, they are issued with a map and given a full rider induction. The induction involves explanation of the map and a run down on the rules at Louee.</p>
<p>Louee has four basic rules.</p>
<ol>
<li>All riders must wear a helmet.</li>
<li>Riders must stay on the traks and off the grass. We do not allow riders to cut tracks or make new ones.</li>
<li>Louee is a one direction riding area. Motocross tracks are anticlockwise and enduro trails have arrows.</li>
<li>Car parks and laneways are srictly first gear slow zones.</li>
</ol>
<p>We also ask all our guests to observe these other rules.</p>
<ol>
<li>All guests enter at their own risk</li>
<li>Bikes are not to be started outside of ride times for any reason.</li>
<li>We ask that all parents supervise their children.</li>
<li>Gates are to be closed if found that way.</li>
<li>Spectators are not permitted on the tracks.</li>
<li>Louee is an Acohol Free Area at all times.</li>
<li>Pets are NOT Permitted at Louee</li>
</ol>

<p>Prior to riding at Louee, please download and <a href="uploads/pdf/2015/Louee-Rules.pdf" target="_blank">read our rules</a>, <a href="uploads/pdf/2015/Waiver-Web.pdf" target="_blank">waiver </a>and if you have a child under 18 riding (without parents/guardian on site) our <a href="uploads/pdf/2015/Deed-Of-Appointment-Of-Temporary-Guardian.pdf" target="_blank">temporay guardians form</a>.</p>
<p>Many thanks and enjoy your time at Louee.</p>

<p>The Louee Enduro and Motocross Complex is on <a href="">Lue Station</a>, a working sheep and cattle property situated near the village of Lue in Central NSW. Louee is only 20 minutes drive East of Mudgee and approximately 3 hours drive from Sydney or Newcastle.</p>

<p>Louee is owned and operated by a bike rider so has all the features you would expect to find at a property that specialises in off road bike riding. Five <a href="https://www.loueemx.com.au/tracks">Motocross Tracks</a>, special areas for juniors and novice riders, 150km of Tracks and Trails designed specifically for off road riding and racing, workshop, canteen, accommodation, bike wash, overnight storage and first aid.</p>

<p>We pride ourselves on giving you a top ride at LOUEE therefore it is <strong>helpful that you </strong>book in advance. Also, plan your ride. Motorcycle riding is dangerous, plan to have a safe ride don't just hope it will happen. Have a look at our <a title="safety-legal" href="safety-legal">safety and legal page</a>&nbsp;and plan your trip.</p>
